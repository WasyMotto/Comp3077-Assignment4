Contact Help

If you need assistance or have any questions about the Destiny 101 website, the contact form is the best way to reach out. Below is a guide to help you use the form correctly:

Contact Form Fields

  • Username: Enter your in-game or website username. This helps us identify who you are.
  • Email: Provide a valid email address so we can respond to your message. Double-check for typos.
  • Message: Write your question, feedback, or issue here. Be as detailed as possible so we can help you effectively.

Examples of Valid Messages

  • Questions about build submissions or guidelines
  • Reporting a bug or error on the site
  • Suggestions for new features or improvements
  • Requesting support for login or account issues

Response Time

We typically respond within 1–3 business days. Please check your spam folder if you don't see a reply.

Need Immediate Help?

If it's urgent or related to account access, include “URGENT” in the subject of your message.
